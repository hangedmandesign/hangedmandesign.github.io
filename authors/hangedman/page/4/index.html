Dirty Rectangles

Dirty Rectangles is an arts collective made for and run by independent game developers, teachers, designers, students, and creators. Initially founded in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada in 2010, Rectangles is one of the original modern game community organizations, and is one of the longest-running. It expanded a chapter to Toronto in 2018. 

Sustainable Game Dev Communities

Sustainable Game Dev Communities is a talk that I wrote and performed at multiple game conferences (Pixelpop Festival in St. Louis, MO, Different Games Conference in Worcester, MA, and several smaller events). The talk details the trials, difficulties, and solutions for creating a sustainable game dev community in any city, at different scales from small to metropolitan.

Chicory: A Colorful Tale

Chicory: A Colorful Tale was a game developed by a small team, led by Greg Lobanov (Wandersong) and with music by Lena Raine (Celeste, Guild Wars 2). It was successfully Kickstarted and picked up by indie publisher Finji (Overland, Night In The Woods). It was released on PC, Mac, and PlayStation platforms in June 2021. It released on Nintendo Switch in December 2021.
