GEMETON is:
A generative fortune-telling toy, a procedural story-telling tool,
a daily ritual game, it's something different for everyone.

It features procedural art, stories, poetry, and fortunes, telling a gentle Divine-Comedy-esque story about learning about the mysteries of the universe.
